° Martian Manhunter "Detective Marciano"
° Name: J'onn J'onnz
° Secret Identity: John Jones, Colorado Police Detective
° Height:  6'7"
° Weight: 250 lbs.
° Hair: None
° Home Planet: Mars
° Species: Green Martian 
° First Appearance: DETECTIVE COMICS #225 (November 1955)
° Essential StoryLines: JLA:Year One, Justice League of America# 144,
Formely Known as the Justice League, Countdown to Infinite Crisis.
° Status: Active. 
° Alliances: Justice League and The Outsiders. 
° Alias : Bloodwynd, Bronze Wraith, Martian Manhunter, 
Fernus, John Jones, Jonh Johnstone, Marco Xavier, Mrs. Klingman,
William Dyer. 
° Martian Powers: Flight, super-strenght, near-invulnerability,
enhanced speed, invisibility, telepathy, shape-shifting, longevity,
which allows John the ability to see trhough solid objets
and over great distances and emit heat energy from 
his eyes "Martian vision". John is vulnerable to flame.
